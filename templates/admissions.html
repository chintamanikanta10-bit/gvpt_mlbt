How to Join Us

A straightforward, parent-friendly admission workflow.

01

Inquiry Submission

Fill out our online inquiry form below or visit the school office to register your interest.

02

Interaction Session

We schedule a friendly interaction session with the child to evaluate their fundamental grade readiness.

03

Documentation

Provide the child's academic reports, Birth Certificate, Aadhaar card copies, and transfer certificates.

04

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment form, finalize standard fees, and collect the child's welcome pack and uniforms.

Required Documents List

Parents must present self-attested copies of the following documents during the interaction session:

  • ✓
    Birth Certificate

    Issued by municipal authority or Gram Panchayat (original verification required).

  • ✓
    Transfer Certificate (TC)

    Counter-signed TC from the previous school (mandatory for Class II and above).

  • ✓
    Aadhaar Card Copy

    Photocopy of the child's Aadhaar along with both parents' Aadhaar cards.

  • ✓
    Photos

    4 recent color passport-sized photos of the student and 2 of each parent.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries regarding admissions, transport, and curriculum.

What is the fee structure for GVP MLBT School? +
As a community-oriented school run by the Gayatri Vidya Parishad trust, we maintain an highly subsidised and affordable fee structure to support underprivileged and working-class families. Specific fee slabs depend on the grade level, and can be reviewed during parent counseling or via office enquiry.
What are the school operating timings? +
Our standard school hours are:
  • Primary Section (LKG to Class V): 8:45 AM to 3:30 PM (Mon - Fri), 8:45 AM to 12:30 PM (Sat).
  • High School Section (Class VI to X): 8:30 AM to 4:00 PM (Mon - Sat).
Is school transport facility provided? +
GVP MLBT School is centrally situated in East Point Colony, making it easily accessible via local city buses and auto-rickshaws. While the school does not run official heavy bus routes, we provide certified van and auto-pool contacts for surrounding localities upon request.
What board curriculum does GVP MLBT follow? +
We follow the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (SSC) curriculum in English medium. In addition, we integrate special modern components such as basic computer programming certification courses, science workshops, and moral values modules.
Are scholarships or fee concessions offered? +
Yes. Following the legacy of our founders advocate M. Jaganadha Rao and Dr. M. Vijaya Lakshmi, GVP Society offers fee concessions and merit-cum-means waivers to deserving candidates from economically weak backgrounds, evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
