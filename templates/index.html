Our Educational Commitment

Established under the aegis of the Mallimadugula Lalithamba Bangarayya (MLB) Trust and managed by the prestigious Gayatri Vidya Parishad (GVP) Society, GVP MLBT School has been a beacon of learning in East Point Colony, Visakhapatnam.

The school specializes in a child-centric syllabus that blends modern academic concepts with strong co-curricular and ethical foundation building. We are dedicated to providing student-centered, skill-oriented instruction, equipping our learners with modern digital capabilities like Infosys Springboard certifications.
