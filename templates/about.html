Our Legacy & History

Tracing four decades of educational dedication and growth.

The Genesis of GVP MLBT

The Gayatri Vidya Parishad Mallimadugula Lalithamba Bangarayya Trust (GVP MLBT) School was created with a profound purpose: to extend the light of qualitative, value-based schooling to kids from underprivileged sectors of Visakhapatnam.

The school was originally conceived by the MLB Trust in 1985, starting class operations on June 19, 1986. With teacher VVN Rao's early foundational guidance, the institution gained recognition from the Government of Andhra Pradesh in 1993.

Understanding the need for sustainable administrative strength, the school subsequently aligned under the stewardship of the prestigious Gayatri Vidya Parishad (GVP) Society. Today, as part of the GVP group, the school benefits from academic collaborations, technical training support, and structural guidance, allowing it to maintain its core community vision while achieving top-tier academic standards.

Our Visionary Founders

Honoring the pioneers who shaped our educational values and ethics.

Driven by Legacy, Guided by Values

GVP MLBT School was brought to life by two outstanding visionaries who dedicated their lives to national welfare and community upliftment:

Advocate M. Jaganadha Rao

Eminent Advocate & Freedom Fighter

A prominent legal expert and freedom fighter, he envisioned education as the strongest instrument to eliminate economic and social inequality.

Dr. M. Vijaya Lakshmi

Social Work Leader & Gandhian Thinker

An exemplary doctor and social reformer, she worked closely with Mahatma Gandhi at the Kasturba Ashram, bringing deep humanitarian concern and moral integrity to the core ethos of our school.

"We do not teach only to impart knowledge, but to establish a robust character in every child."

Our Vision

To stand as a model institution that provides quality, accessible, and skill-driven primary-secondary education. We aim to enable every child from the surrounding areas of Visakhapatnam to break barriers, master modern digital competencies, and build a successful, morally sound future.

🚀

Our Mission

We commit to delivering comprehensive classroom learning combined with certified digital education, rich science/computer lab exposure, and sports. We seek to foster moral foundations, teach environment-friendly practices, and support parents through inclusive fees and counseling.
