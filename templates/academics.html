Our Curriculum Structure

A balanced curriculum focused on strong conceptual foundations and academic rigor.

Primary School (LKG - Class V)

Ages 4 - 10

Our primary division lays a playful yet structured groundwork for young learners. The emphasis is on language command (English and regional Telugu/Hindi), elementary arithmetic logic, environmental awareness, and basic social skills.

  • ✔️ Active play-way and activity-based learning approaches
  • ✔️ Standardized foundational mathematics and spelling drills
  • ✔️ Integrated arts, crafts, storytelling, and indoor games
  • ✔️ Monthly developmental reviews instead of stressful examinations

High School (Class VI - Class X)

Ages 11 - 15

The high school curriculum follows the State Board of Secondary Education (SSC), Andhra Pradesh. It prepares children for standard public board examinations, with specialized classes in Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Advanced Mathematics, Social Studies, and English.

  • ✔️ Rigorous practice tests, assignments, and science laboratory experiments
  • ✔️ Systematic board examination revision planning workshops
  • ✔️ Focus on computer science fundamentals and logical problem-solving
  • ✔️ Active guidance classes for higher education path selection

Special Skill Development

Integrating digital literacy and critical preparedness into standard curricula.

💻

Infosys Springboard Certifications

In collaboration with GVP technical mentors, our students are enrolled in specialized courses via the Infosys Springboard platform. Through structured digital curriculum access, high schoolers earn certifications in computer programming basics, office productivity tools, and web design concepts, ensuring they possess high-value IT skills.

📝

Board Exam Strategy Workshops

To support Class X students in scoring high board marks, we organize regular exam preparation workshops. Renowned experts and senior GVP academicians guide students on time management, answer structuring, common grading traps, and methods to maintain calm during exams.

🧘

Co-Curricular & Ethics Modules

Weekly interactive sessions are dedicated to moral education and ethics. Drawing inspiration from Mahatma Gandhi's life (reflecting our founder Dr. M. Vijaya Lakshmi's legacy), students engage in discussions on civic duties, honesty, non-violence, and community support.

Beyond the Classroom

Fostering visual creativity, physical agility, and artistic talents.

Sports & Athletics

Volleyball, Kabaddi, badminton, and athletic track training. We promote physical fitness, teamwork, and athletic sportsmanship.

Art & Craft

Painting, clay molding, origami, and sketching workshops to nurture students' artistic design sense and manual dexterity.

Music & Drama

Cultural dance training, classical music lessons, and stage performances, showcased during Annual Day celebrations.
